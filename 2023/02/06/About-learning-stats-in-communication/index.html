About learning stats in communication

2023-02-06

After the SEM course instructed by my supervisor, some of other students began to ask me some questions about stats. Recently, I’m helping one of my peers in doing SEM model, regression, mediation, and moderation. After I taught them some of the basic stats, I found actually there’re lots of similar questions and they seems to lack a holistic understanding about the basic stats that are commenly used in communication. So I have been planning to write some notes about stats based on my own understanding and hopefully, these tutorials may be helpful to learn the basic stats in communication.
