《气候在变化，那么社会呢》

《气候变化：我与我女儿的对话》

This books is about basic knowledge of climate change, written in the form of conversations between a father and his daughter.

  • Climate ≠≠= weather.
  • Climate change refers to the change of average temperature.
  • Scientific consensus.
  • Greenhouse effect. But it is a natural phenomenon supporting human’s lives. Therefore, it is not naturally bad. (Complex knowledge for lay person perhaps.)
  • Meat is a source of cliamte change. Food, electricity, etc., all require fossil fuels.
  • CO2 ⇒\Rightarrow⇒ atmosphere + forest + sea. Therefore, the increase in the atmosphere is not that much.
  • Disasters: more diseases, more raining, wildfires.
  • Last generation causes the problem but they don’t need to bear the consequences.
  • Challges to build the new world + adaptation to the change of the world.
